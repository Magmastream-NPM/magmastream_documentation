## ❓ What is Magmastream?

---

Magmastream is the perfect bridge between your bot and Lavalink, designed to make playing music on Discord a better experience than ever before. Whether you're creating a simple music bot for your friends or for thousands of servers.

It is built on an event-driven architecture, giving you precise control over everything from node connections to song playback. With a modular plugin system, an integrated database for data persistence, and extensive support for Lavalink plugins, Magmastream is designed for simplicity and scalability.
