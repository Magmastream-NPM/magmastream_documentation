> Represents synced or unsynced lyrics data returned by Lavalink plugins.
> 
> | <i className="fa-solid fa-hashtag"/> Property | <i className="fa-solid fa-font"/> Type                                                              | <i className="fa-solid fa-list"/> Description          | <i className="fa-solid fa-flask"/> Optional |
> |:----------------------------------------------|:----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|:-------------------------------------------------------|:-------------------------------------------:|
> | `name`                                        | [string](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/JavaScript/Reference/Global_Objects/String)   | Source or provider name of the lyrics.                 | <i className="fa-solid fa-xmark"/>          |
> | `synced`                                      | [boolean](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/JavaScript/Reference/Global_Objects/Boolean) | Whether the lyrics are time-synced (karaoke-style).    | <i className="fa-solid fa-xmark"/>          |
> | `data`                                        | [object[]](#data)                                                                                   | Array of lyric line objects with optional timing info. | <i className="fa-solid fa-xmark"/>          |
> | `rtl`                                         | [boolean](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/JavaScript/Reference/Global_Objects/Boolean) | Whether the lyrics should be rendered right-to-left.   | <i className="fa-solid fa-xmark"/>          |

### `data`

> Array of lyric line objects with optional timing info.
> 
> | <i className="fa-solid fa-hashtag"/> Property | <i className="fa-solid fa-font"/> Type                                                            | <i className="fa-solid fa-list"/> Description | <i className="fa-solid fa-flask"/> Optional |
> |:----------------------------------------------|:--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|:----------------------------------------------|:-------------------------------------------:|
> | `startTime`                                   | [number](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/JavaScript/Reference/Global_Objects/Number) | Start time of the lyric line in milliseconds. | <i className="fa-solid fa-check"/>          |
> | `endTime`                                     | [number](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/JavaScript/Reference/Global_Objects/Number) | End time of the lyric line in milliseconds.   | <i className="fa-solid fa-check"/>          |
> | `text`                                        | [string](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/JavaScript/Reference/Global_Objects/String) | The actual lyric text.                        | <i className="fa-solid fa-xmark"/>          |
