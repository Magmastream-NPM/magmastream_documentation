> Represents the event emitted when a player's volume is adjusted.
> 
> | <i className="fa-solid fa-hashtag"/> Property | <i className="fa-solid fa-font"/> Type                                                                      | <i className="fa-solid fa-list"/> Description             | <i className="fa-solid fa-flask"/> Optional |
> |:--------------------------------------------- |:------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|:--------------------------------------------------------- |:-------------------------------------------:|
> | `type`                                        | [`"volume"`](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/JavaScript/Reference/Global_Objects/String)       | Constant string indicating the event type.                | <i className="fa-solid fa-xmark"/>          |
> | `action`                                      | [`"adjust"`](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/JavaScript/Reference/Global_Objects/String)       | The action performed on the volume.                       | <i className="fa-solid fa-xmark"/>          |
> | `previousVolume`                              | [number](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/JavaScript/Reference/Global_Objects/Number) \| `null` | The volume level before the change, or `null` if unknown. | <i className="fa-solid fa-check"/>          |
> | `currentVolume`                               | [number](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/JavaScript/Reference/Global_Objects/Number) \| `null` | The volume level after the change, or `null` if unknown.  | <i className="fa-solid fa-check"/>          |
