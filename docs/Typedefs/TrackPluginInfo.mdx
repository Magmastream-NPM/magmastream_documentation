> Represents optional metadata added to a track by plugins.
> 
> | <i className="fa-solid fa-hashtag"/> Property      | <i className="fa-solid fa-font"/> Type                                                                   | <i className="fa-solid fa-list"/> Description                                   | <i className="fa-solid fa-flask"/> Optional |
> |:--------------------------------------------------|:---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|:-------------------------------------------------------------------------------|:-------------------------------------------:|
> | `albumName`                                       | [string](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/JavaScript/Reference/Global_Objects/String)         | Name of the album the track belongs to.                                        | <i className="fa-solid fa-check"/>          |
> | `albumUrl`                                        | [string](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/JavaScript/Reference/Global_Objects/String)         | URL to the album’s page or artwork.                                           | <i className="fa-solid fa-check"/>          |
> | `artistArtworkUrl`                                | [string](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/JavaScript/Reference/Global_Objects/String)         | URL to the artist’s artwork or image.                                         | <i className="fa-solid fa-check"/>          |
> | `artistUrl`                                       | [string](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/JavaScript/Reference/Global_Objects/String)         | URL to the artist’s page.                                                     | <i className="fa-solid fa-check"/>          |
> | `isPreview`                                       | [string](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/JavaScript/Reference/Global_Objects/String)         | Indicates if the track is a preview version.                                  | <i className="fa-solid fa-check"/>          |
> | `previewUrl`                                      | [string](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/JavaScript/Reference/Global_Objects/String)         | URL to a preview audio file or snippet.                                       | <i className="fa-solid fa-check"/>          |

---

> Used in the [`Track`](./Track.mdx) interface’s `pluginInfo` property.
