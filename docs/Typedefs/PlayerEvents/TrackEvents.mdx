> These events are triggered during the lifecycle of track playback — from start, to end, to error handling.  
> They are useful for reacting to playback changes or handling track failures gracefully.  
> These interfaces extend [`BaseEvent`](./BaseEvent.mdx).

### `TrackStartEvent`

> Emitted when a track begins playback.  
> Extends: [`BaseEvent`](./BaseEvent.mdx).
> 
> | <i className="fa-solid fa-hashtag"/> Property | <i className="fa-solid fa-font"/> Type                                                            | <i className="fa-solid fa-list"/> Description | <i className="fa-solid fa-flask"/> Optional |
> |:----------------------------------------------|:--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|:----------------------------------------------|:-------------------------------------------:|
> | `op`                                          | `"event"`                                                                                         | Lavalink operation type.                      | <i className="fa-solid fa-xmark"/>          |
> | `type`                                        | `"TrackStartEvent"`                                                                               | Event type identifier.                        | <i className="fa-solid fa-xmark"/>          |
> | `guildId`                                     | [string](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/JavaScript/Reference/Global_Objects/String) | Guild this event applies to.                  | <i className="fa-solid fa-xmark"/>          |
> | `track`                                       | [`TrackData`](../TrackData.mdx)                                                                   | Track that started playing.                   | <i className="fa-solid fa-xmark"/>          |

---

> |:----------------------------------------------|:--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|:----------------------------------------------|:-------------------------------------------:|
> | `op`                                          | `"event"`                                                                                         | Lavalink operation type.                      | <i className="fa-solid fa-xmark"/>          |
> | `type`                                        | `"TrackEndEvent"`                                                                                 | Event type identifier.                        | <i className="fa-solid fa-xmark"/>          |
> | `guildId`                                     | [string](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/JavaScript/Reference/Global_Objects/String) | Guild this event applies to.                  | <i className="fa-solid fa-xmark"/>          |

### `TrackStuckEvent`

> Emitted when Lavalink fails to decode audio frames within the given threshold — often indicating a corrupted or inaccessible track.  
> Extends: [`BaseEvent`](./BaseEvent.mdx).
> 
> | <i className="fa-solid fa-hashtag"/> Property | <i className="fa-solid fa-font"/> Type                                                            | <i className="fa-solid fa-list"/> Description                     | <i className="fa-solid fa-flask"/> Optional |
> |:----------------------------------------------|:--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|:------------------------------------------------------------------|:-------------------------------------------:|
> | `op`                                          | `"event"`                                                                                         | Lavalink operation type.                                          | <i className="fa-solid fa-xmark"/>          |
> | `type`                                        | `"TrackStuckEvent"`                                                                               | Event type identifier.                                            | <i className="fa-solid fa-xmark"/>          |
> | `guildId`                                     | [string](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/JavaScript/Reference/Global_Objects/String) | Guild this event applies to.                                      | <i className="fa-solid fa-xmark"/>          |
> | `thresholdMs`                                 | [number](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/JavaScript/Reference/Global_Objects/Number) | Threshold in milliseconds Lavalink waited before reporting stuck. | <i className="fa-solid fa-xmark"/>          |
