> All Lavalink events inherit from this base event structure.  
> You can use this structure to narrow or type-check incoming player events regardless of their specific payload.

### PlayerEvent

> Base interface shared across all Lavalink player events.
> 
> | <i className="fa-solid fa-hashtag"/> Property | <i className="fa-solid fa-font"/> Type                                                            | <i className="fa-solid fa-list"/> Description | <i className="fa-solid fa-flask"/> Optional |
> |:----------------------------------------------|:--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|:----------------------------------------------|:-------------------------------------------:|
> | `op`                                          | [string](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/JavaScript/Reference/Global_Objects/String) = "event"                                                                                      | Lavalink operation identifier.                | <i className="fa-solid fa-xmark"/>          |
> | `type`                                        | [`PlayerEventType`](#playereventtype)                                                             | Specific event type.                          | <i className="fa-solid fa-xmark"/>          |
> | `guildId`                                     | [string](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/JavaScript/Reference/Global_Objects/String) | Guild this event refers to.                   | <i className="fa-solid fa-xmark"/>          |

---

### PlayerEventType

> Union of all possible event types that may be emitted by the Lavalink player instance.

```ts
type PlayerEventType =
  | "TrackStartEvent"
  | "TrackEndEvent"
  | "TrackExceptionEvent"
  | "TrackStuckEvent"
  | "WebSocketClosedEvent"
  | "SegmentSkipped"
  | "SegmentsLoaded"
  | "ChaptersLoaded"
  | "ChapterStarted"
  | "LyricsFound"
  | "LyricsNotFound"
  | "LyricsLine";
