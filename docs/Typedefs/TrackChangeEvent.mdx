> The event emitted when a player's track changes. This includes changes such as starting, ending, autoplaying, or skipping to the previous track.
>

> | `type`                                            | [`"track"`](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/JavaScript/Reference/Global_Objects/String)                             | The event type identifier. Always `"track"` for this event.                 | <i className="fa-solid fa-xmark"/>          |
> | `action`                                          | [`"start"`](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/JavaScript/Reference/Global_Objects/String) \| [`"end"`](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/JavaScript/Reference/Global_Objects/String) \| [`"previous"`](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/JavaScript/Reference/Global_Objects/String) \| [`"timeUpdate"`](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/JavaScript/Reference/Global_Objects/String) \| [`"autoPlay"`](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/JavaScript/Reference/Global_Objects/String) | Describes the specific action that caused the track event.                  | <i className="fa-solid fa-xmark"/>          |
