> Represents the configuration object for connecting to a Lavalink or Nodelink node.  
> These options control connection, retries, resume behavior, and priority.
> 
> | <i className="fa-solid fa-hashtag" /> Property      | <i className="fa-solid fa-font" /> Type                                                              | <i className="fa-solid fa-list" /> Description                                                  | <i className="fa-solid fa-flask" /> Optional |
> |:----------------------------------------------------|:------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|:---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |:--------------------------------------------:|
> | `host`                                              | [string](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/JavaScript/Reference/Global_Objects/String)     | The hostname or IP address of the Lavalink node.                                               | <i className="fa-solid fa-xmark" />          |
> | `port`                                              | [number](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/JavaScript/Reference/Global_Objects/Number)     | Port used to connect to the node.                                                              | <i className="fa-solid fa-check" />          |
> | `password`                                          | [string](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/JavaScript/Reference/Global_Objects/String)     | Password required to authenticate with the node.                                               | <i className="fa-solid fa-check" />          |
> | `useSSL`                                            | [boolean](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/JavaScript/Reference/Global_Objects/Boolean)   | If `true`, connects over HTTPS/WSS.                                                            | <i className="fa-solid fa-check" />          |
> | `identifier`                                        | [string](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/JavaScript/Reference/Global_Objects/String)     | A unique identifier for this node. Helpful for logging or cluster setups.                      | <i className="fa-solid fa-check" />          |
> | `maxRetryAttempts`                                  | [number](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/JavaScript/Reference/Global_Objects/Number)     | Number of reconnection attempts before giving up.                                              | <i className="fa-solid fa-check" />          |
> | `retryDelayMs`                                      | [number](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/JavaScript/Reference/Global_Objects/Number)     | Milliseconds between reconnection attempts.                                                    | <i className="fa-solid fa-check" />          |
> | `enableSessionResumeOption`                         | [boolean](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/JavaScript/Reference/Global_Objects/Boolean)   | Whether the node should resume existing players on reconnect.                                  | <i className="fa-solid fa-check" />          |
> | `sessionTimeoutMs`                                  | [number](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/JavaScript/Reference/Global_Objects/Number)     | Time (in seconds) the server should wait before removing a disconnected session.               | <i className="fa-solid fa-check" />          |
> | `apiRequestTimeoutMs`                               | [number](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/JavaScript/Reference/Global_Objects/Number)     | Request timeout for REST API calls to the node.                                                | <i className="fa-solid fa-check" />          |
> | `nodePriority`                                      | [number](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/JavaScript/Reference/Global_Objects/Number)     | Determines the priority of the node when selecting nodes dynamically. Higher = more preferred. | <i className="fa-solid fa-check" />          |
> | `isNodeLink`                                        | [boolean](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/JavaScript/Reference/Global_Objects/Boolean)   | Whether the node is a NodeLink node.                                                           | <i className="fa-solid fa-check" />          |
> | `isBackup`                                          | [boolean](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/JavaScript/Reference/Global_Objects/Boolean)   | Whether the node is a backup node.                                                             | <i className="fa-solid fa-check" />          |
