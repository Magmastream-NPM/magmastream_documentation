>

> | `author`                                     | [string](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/JavaScript/Reference/Global_Objects/String) | Author of the track                                                            | <i className="fa-solid fa-xmark"/>           |

> | `artworkUrl`                                 | [string](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/JavaScript/Reference/Global_Objects/String) | Direct link to the artwork or thumbnail                                        | <i className="fa-solid fa-check"/>           |
> | `sourceName`                                 | [`TrackSourceName`](./TrackSourceName.mdx)                                                    | Enum representing the track source (YouTube, SoundCloud, etc.)                 | <i className="fa-solid fa-check"/>           |
