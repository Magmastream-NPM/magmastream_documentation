> Represents a playable audio track used internally by the player. Includes metadata, playback options, and plugin data.
>

> |:----------------------------------------------|:-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|:----------------------------------------------------------- |:-------------------------------------------:|
> | `track`                                       | [string](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/JavaScript/Reference/Global_Objects/String)                                                                  | Base64-encoded track string.                                | <i className="fa-solid fa-xmark"/>          |
> | `artworkUrl`                                  | [string](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/JavaScript/Reference/Global_Objects/String)                                                                  | URL to the track's artwork image.                           | <i className="fa-solid fa-xmark"/>          |
> | `sourceName`                                  | `"AppleMusic"` \| `"Bandcamp"` \| `"Deezer"` \| `"Jiosaavn"` \| `"Qobuz"` \| `"SoundCloud"` \| `"Spotify"` \| `"Tidal"` \| `"VKMusic"` \| `"YouTube"`              | The source platform of the track.                           | <i className="fa-solid fa-xmark"/>          |
> | `title`                                       | [string](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/JavaScript/Reference/Global_Objects/String)                                                                  | Title of the track.                                         | <i className="fa-solid fa-xmark"/>          |
> | `identifier`                                  | [string](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/JavaScript/Reference/Global_Objects/String)                                                                  | Unique identifier of the track (e.g., YouTube video ID).    | <i className="fa-solid fa-xmark"/>          |

> | `isrc`                                        | [string](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/JavaScript/Reference/Global_Objects/String)                                                                  | ISRC (International Standard Recording Code) for the track. | <i className="fa-solid fa-xmark"/>          |
> | `isSeekable`                                  | [boolean](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/JavaScript/Reference/Global_Objects/Boolean)                                                                | Indicates whether the track can be seeked.                  | <i className="fa-solid fa-xmark"/>          |
> | `isStream`                                    | [boolean](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/JavaScript/Reference/Global_Objects/Boolean)                                                                | Indicates whether the track is a live stream.               | <i className="fa-solid fa-xmark"/>          |

> | `displayThumbnail(size?)`                     | [`Sizes`](./Sizes.mdx)                                                                                                                                             | Returns a track thumbnail URL with optional resolution.     | <i className="fa-solid fa-check"/>          |
